How It Works

From URL to smart link in three steps

No configuration dashboards. No manual setup. Just paste, customize, and go.

01

Paste Your Destination URL

Drop in any link — YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, Telegram, Google Maps, X, or any custom URL. DeepLinkOS handles the rest automatically.

02

Platform is Auto-Detected

The engine reads your URL instantly, identifies the platform, shows the brand icon, pre-fills the slug intelligently, and sets all routing fields — zero manual input.

03

Compose & Share Your Link

Click Compose Link. Your short link is generated, ready to copy and test. It opens the native app on mobile and falls back gracefully to the web on desktop.

Features

Everything built in,
nothing to configure

DeepLinkOS handles the hard parts of deep linking so you focus on sending people to the right place.

🧠

Instant Platform Detection

Paste any URL and DeepLinkOS identifies the platform in real time — YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, Telegram, Google Maps, X, or any custom URI. No dropdowns needed.

⚡

Auto Slug Inference

Slugs are derived directly from the URL. A YouTube channel becomes its handle, a Telegram channel becomes its username, a WhatsApp number becomes the link — all automatic.

📱

iOS & Android Routing

One link intelligently routes iOS users to the native app, Android to theirs, and desktop users to the web — all from a single short URL with no client-side logic needed.

🔗

Editable Custom Slugs

Keep the auto-inferred slug or type your own. Your short link second half is yours to own, brand, and share. Clean, memorable, no random gibberish.

🛡️

Graceful Fallbacks

If an app isn't installed, users fall back to the App Store, Play Store, or a desktop URL in priority order. No dead ends, no broken experiences, ever.

🚀

Localhost-Ready Testing

Every generated link routes through localhost:3000 during development. Compose a link here and Test Link opens the real redirect instantly.

Smart Routing

One link.
Every device covered.

When someone taps your DeepLinkOS short link, the router reads their device's User-Agent and sends them to the ideal destination — no redirector apps, no friction.

The fallback chain ensures no user hits a dead end. iOS checks deep link → App Store → web. Android mirrors the same. Desktop goes straight to the URL.
